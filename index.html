About Me

Engineer built for the built environment

I'm Ravikula Silva, an electrical engineer with a genuine passion for building services. With an MSc from RMIT University (Distinction, GPA 3.6/4) and a BSc from the University of Moratuwa, I bring analytical rigour and practical depth to electrical building services design, estimation, and commissioning.

At iAutomation Pty Ltd I contributed to the full design-to-commissioning cycle across residential and commercial projects, including supporting estimation and delivery of a healthcare facility lighting control system valued at AUD 120K. I maintain SLDs and shop drawings in AutoCAD, and program KNX/ETS systems delivering DALI dimming, scene control, daylight harvesting, relay switching, and BMS interfacing. My RMIT building services design project, covering maximum demand calculations, cable sizing, and protection coordination per AS 3000 and AS 3008.1, achieved a voltage drop of 1.25% against a 3.35% permissible limit, with lighting system designed according to AS 1680 using DIALux.

I am a Graduate Member of Engineers Australia (GradIEAust), hold a Construction White Card, and am actively targeting electrical building services, lighting design, and building automation roles across Australia.
